About Us

GOLDAGRIC NIGERIA LIMITED is an indigenous company incorporated in 1997 (RC316813) as a limited liability company. The company has broad- base specialist teams executing projects on various development domains leveraging our strong input dealership and network of outlets. It is a reputable representative of major manufactures in its class. Goldagric Nigeria Limited has established itself as a trusted consultant on a growing range of services that support Government and notable private/development institutions. GoldAgric enjoys considerable level of goodwill and collaboration with reputable private and Government or non-Governmental organizations throughout the country, and abroad.
